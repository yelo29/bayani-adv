Quick Guide

  • Add Product: Click "Add New Product" to upload a new product with image
  • Edit Product: Click "Edit" to modify product details (image is optional)
  • Delete Product: Click "Delete" to remove a product (also deletes the image file)
  • Tag Class: Used for styling - choose: home, fashion, food, or decor
  • Sector: Category for filtering - choose: Home Goods, Apparel, Pantry Items, or Decor / Handicrafts
  • Images: Uploaded to uploads/products/ folder
