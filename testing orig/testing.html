Why Gawang Pinas

Every product has a story worth telling.

Gawang Pinas is a platform dedicated to shining a light on the incredible products being made by Filipino hands — in small workshops, family kitchens, and community cooperatives across all 7,641 islands.

We don't sell. We celebrate. We want every Filipino — and the world — to discover the quality and creativity right in their own backyard.
